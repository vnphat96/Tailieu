Suy ra $\underset{(0 ; 4 \pi)}{\text{SĐCT}}\{ f(u)\}=\underset{(0 ; 4 \pi)}{\text{SĐCT}}\{u\}+\text{SNBL}\left\{\hoac{&u=1 \\ &u=-2}\right\}=3+8=11$.
}
\end{ex}
