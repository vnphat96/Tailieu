			\itemch Vì $\tan \alpha = \dfrac{1}{\cot \alpha} =- \dfrac{4}{3}$ nên $P=3\tan \alpha - 2 \cot \alpha = 3\cdot \left(-\dfrac{4}{3}\right) - 2\cdot \left(-\dfrac{3}{4}\right) = -\dfrac{5}{2}$.
